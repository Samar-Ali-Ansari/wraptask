WrapTask : A Todo App

1. Type your task in the Input field stated as "Add your task here!" and click "Add Task" to add it to the list.

2. Click "Reset" next to "Add" to reset all task from list as well as from your browser storage

3. Use the "Insert", "Enter", "Shift" key to quickly focus on the input field.

4. Use the "Delete" key to quickly clear all tasks.

5. Click on a task to toggle its completion status.

6. Click on "Contact" to learn more about the creator.

7.Click on the sidebar from the nav menu to quickly traverse around the TodoApp if you're using a mobile device

8. Don't worry about loosing your tasks. Even after refreshing your tasks will remain untouched


Don't forget to visit the Creator profiles from navbar as well as from the sidebar
